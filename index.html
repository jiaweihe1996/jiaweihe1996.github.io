About Me

I am Jiawei He, a second year PhD student in BRAVE group of Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Zhaoxiang Zhang. Before this I got my BS degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2019. During my undergraduate years, I was a research intern in Institute of Artificial Intellegence and Robotics in XJTU from 2017 to 2019.

I am also a research intern at TuSimple, mentored by Zehao Huang and Naiyan Wang, focusing on Multiple Object Tracking.

Research Interests

I am interested in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Combinatorial Optimization, and especially human-centered computer vision problems and video analysis.
